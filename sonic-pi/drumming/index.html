Drumming (Sonic Pi)

After Steve Reich (1970-71) — audio via Sonic Pi over OSC

Back to Sonic Pi

Four instrumental parts — bongos, marimbas, glockenspiels, and all combined — each with interlocking voices playing the same 12-beat rhythmic pattern at different phase offsets. Voices enter and drop out based on a density control, and pattern rotation creates constantly shifting accents.
