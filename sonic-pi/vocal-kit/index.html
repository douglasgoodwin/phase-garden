Vocal Kit (Sonic Pi)

Phasing beatbox — three mouth-sound drum kits drifting apart

Back to Sonic Pi

Three drum kits made entirely of mouth sounds — kicks, snares, hi-hats, clicks, pops, toms, shakers, and crashes — play the same 16-step pattern. Kit 1 holds steady while Kits 2 and 3 drift ahead at increasing rates, creating polyrhythmic textures as the copies slide against each other. Piano Phase for a beatbox.

Setup
